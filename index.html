To My Mum ❤️

Mum, I don’t always say it the way I should, but you are my first home, my safest place, and my forever blessing. Before I understood the world, I understood your love — soft, constant, and unconditional.

You’ve held me in your heart even when I couldn’t stand on my own. You’ve prayed for me in silence, worried in ways I may never fully understand, and still smiled just to make my days lighter.

If I ever become even half of the strength, kindness, and love you are, then I will have done something right in this life.

Thank you for every sacrifice, every late night, every “I’m fine” when I know you weren’t. I see you. I appreciate you. I love you more than words will ever be able to carry.

No matter where life takes me, I will always be your child, and you will always be my greatest love in human form.

Forever yours 🤍
